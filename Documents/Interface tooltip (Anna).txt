An interface tooltip (window with text).

1.Tooltips can contain various information, such as the object's name, description, characteristics, important details, and instructions for interaction. 
2.Interface tooltips can serve different purposes, such as providing information about the health and mana of characters, item descriptions, warnings about hostile objects, or gameplay tips.
3.The primary goal of interface tooltips is to simplify the player's interaction with the game world by making information more accessible and understandable.
